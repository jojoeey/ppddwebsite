Passion for Pet, Commitment to Care

Founded in 2022, Pupu & Dudu Pet Store began as a small online business by a passionate young couple, Ms. Yau Li Xuan Brenda and her partner. Inspired by their love for animals, they set out to make quality pet care more accessible to everyone in Malaysia. Starting from Shopee and social media, Pupu & Dudu quickly grew into a trusted name, offering a wide range of pet products and services while occasionally engaging with the community through booth events.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, safe, and affordable pet products and services while promoting responsible pet ownership.

We strive to encourage more people to love and care for animals, making pet care convenient and accessible to every pet owner.

Our Vision

We aim to be Malaysia’s trusted pet supply brand, building a strong community of pet lovers who value animal welfare and responsible care.

Our commitment is to grow alongside our customers, contributing positively to the well-being of pets everywhere.
